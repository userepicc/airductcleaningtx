Why Plano Homes Need Professional Duct Cleaning

Plano is one of the most affluent and well-maintained cities in the DFW Metroplex, but even premium homes accumulate significant contaminants in their HVAC ductwork over time. The Collin County suburb experiences the same extreme summer heat as Dallas (96–102°F), with HVAC systems running 10–14 hours daily from May through October.

Many Plano homes, particularly in established neighborhoods like Deerfield, Willow Bend, and Kings Gate, were built during the city's rapid growth in the 1980s and 1990s. These 30–40 year old duct systems have accumulated decades of dust, particulate matter, pet dander, and potential microbial growth that degrade indoor air quality (IAQ).

Newer developments near Legacy West and the Frisco border feature more modern HVAC systems, but construction debris and drywall dust from ongoing Collin County development still necessitate professional cleaning. Our NADCA-certified team uses HEPA filtration technology to deliver thorough allergen reduction for Plano homes of all ages.
