Why It Matters

Why Air Duct Cleaning Is Essential in the Texas Climate

Texas homeowners face unique indoor air quality (IAQ) challenges. With extreme summer heat driving HVAC systems to run 8–12 hours daily, combined with high humidity levels that foster microbial growth and elevated pollen counts, your ductwork becomes a reservoir for allergens and contaminants.

The EPA estimates that indoor air can be 2 to 5 times more polluted than outdoor air. In the Texas climate, where homes remain sealed for months during summer, regular duct cleaning isn't a luxury—it's a necessity for allergen reduction and healthy living.
