About Air Duct Cleaning TX

15+ years of trusted expertise delivering superior indoor air quality to homes and businesses across the Lone Star State.

Our Story

Built on Trust, Driven by Clean Air

Air Duct Cleaning TX was founded in 2010 with a simple but powerful mission: to help Texas families breathe healthier indoor air. What started as a small team of HVAC professionals has grown into one of the state's most trusted indoor air quality companies, serving communities from Dallas to Houston and everywhere in between.

Our founder recognized that Texas homeowners face unique indoor air quality (IAQ) challenges — extreme heat driving extended HVAC usage, high humidity fostering microbial growth, and elevated pollen counts filling ductwork with allergens. Traditional HVAC companies weren't addressing these challenges with the specialized expertise they demanded.

Today, our team of NADCA-certified technicians has cleaned over 50,000 duct systems across Texas. We've invested in the best equipment — including truck-mounted HEPA filtration systems and negative-pressure technology — because we believe every Texas family deserves access to professional-grade air quality solutions.
